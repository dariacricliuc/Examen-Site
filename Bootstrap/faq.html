Frequently Asked Questions

What is Contemporary Japanese Art?

It encompasses a wide range of styles and media, from traditional nihonga and ukiyo-e influences to modern pop art, installation and digital media, reflecting Japan's unique cultural fusion.

Who are the most famous modern Japanese artists?

Key figures include Yayoi Kusama (known for dots), Takashi Murakami (Superflat style) and Yoshitomo Nara, among many others featured on our Artists page.

Can I use the images from this project?

This is an educational project. Most images are used for study purposes; for commercial use, you should contact the original artists or copyright holders.

Is this project affiliated with any museum?

No, this is a student project developed for USARB, created to explore and showcase the beauty of Japanese contemporary culture.

How can I stay updated on new artists?

You can subscribe to our newsletter in the footer or follow our social media links to get the latest news about ZenArt.
